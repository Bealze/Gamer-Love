Commands:

!mirror (will repeat text back to you in the chat box) 
example: !mirror Hello World
!joinf # (force joins a faction without needing server restart. replace # with 3-7, matching faction numbers in hosting.cfg) 
example: !joinf 4
!mypos (Will give you your current Coordinates  in the chat box.)
example: !mypos
Example Output: 4368.2 1298.1 21.1
